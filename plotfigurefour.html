Wind Speed

As seen in the above graph, there are a couple of patterns emerging in the data concerning wind speed. First, the most extreme wind speeds are found in the extreme northern latitudes. Next, the calmest winds are found near the equator, specifically between 0 nd -15 degrees. Lastly, the majority of wind speeds, regardless of latitude, are below 15 mph.
